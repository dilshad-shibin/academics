Ma'din Arts & Science College

Ma'din Arts & Science College is the fulfillment of another long-cherished dream of Ma'din Academy in the field of higher education. The college established in 2008 started functioning in the academic year 2009-10. It is a self financing institution affiliated to University of Calicut and Recognized by the Government of Kerala. The College now offers Four undergraduate courses namely BA English Language and Literature, BSc Microbiology, BBA and B.Com Computer Application. Located at Edupark, Melmuri, the college of nestled away from the chaos of the city in an atmosphere that is conductive to the pursuit of knowledge. It is surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty and green environment. As a result of the team work of qualified and experienced faculty, the college was very successfull in maintaining high academic standards, results and descipline in the previous years.
